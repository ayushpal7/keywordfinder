Keyword Research Guide

The Complete Guide to Keyword Research in 2025

Keyword research is the foundation of every successful SEO strategy. The keywords you choose determine whether you get traffic or get ignored by Google.

What Is Keyword Difficulty (KD)?

KD is scored 0–100 based on how competitive the top-10 ranking pages are. Below 40 is easy, 40–69 medium, 70+ hard. New websites should target KD below 30 first.

The 4 Types of Search Intent

Informational — user wants to learn. Best for blog posts. Commercial — researching before buying. Best for comparison content. Transactional — ready to buy. Best for product pages. Navigational — looking for a specific site.

Long-Tail Keywords: Your Secret Weapon

Long-tail keywords (4+ words) have lower competition and higher conversion rates. They account for over 70% of all searches. New sites should focus on long-tail first to build traffic momentum.

CPC and Content Value

High CPC keywords indicate strong commercial value. Even for organic SEO, high-CPC keywords signal profitable territory worth targeting with content.

Topic Cluster Strategy

Create one comprehensive pillar page on a broad topic, then cluster pages on subtopics — all linking to each other. This builds topical authority and helps all pages rank higher.
