Our Story

Why KeywordFinder.dev Exists

In 2024, Ayush was working on a side project and needed keyword data. Ahrefs wanted $99/month. SEMrush wanted $119/month. Even "free" tools gave 3 searches per day with crippled data.

So he built his own. Powered by Google Gemini AI with Groq as fallback — both completely free APIs — the tool generates real, usable keyword data with no limits.

What started as a personal tool got shared with friends, then the SEO community. Today, KeywordFinder.dev is used by bloggers, freelancers, students, and startup founders across India and beyond.
